Active Response Center: 
EU based privacy first solution to fight Covid-19

 

The answer clearly lies in data, in tracking the spread of the disease, allowing for rapid response, targeted testing and quarantine. We managed to build it under the EU privacy rules, based on user consent.

How It Works?
What we can visualize?

  1. Patient calls the center and gives consent to access their data (both the app and cell-phone/banking)
  2. The operator uses the data to rapidly build list of potential exposures
  3. Exposed contacts receive warning and request for testing

How italian city Vo' in Venice got success using smart quarantine?

  1. Wide testing experiment in a town of 3,300 shows the importance of testing and quarantine of those who are infected.
  2. In the first wave, when the whole population got tested, they identified 3% of the population to be infected, with half being symptoms free.
  3. In a second wave 10 days later and after quarantine of the infected, the number dropped to 0.3%
  4. The second wave identified at least 6 individuals without symptoms that also went into quarantine.
